Chaching Contact & Return Policy

Contact Policy:
For any questions, orders, or product enquiries, customers can reach us via:

  • Instagram: @chaching_official

  • Email: contact@chaching.co.za

Please allow 24–48 hours for a response. Messages sent outside of business hours will be addressed the next working day. Kindly include your order number and full name when contacting us for faster assistance.

Return & Refund Policy:

  • Returns are accepted within 7 days of purchase, provided the item is unworn, unused, and in original packaging.

  • Proof of purchase is required for all returns or exchanges.

  • Refunds will be issued only if the item is defective or incorrect due to our error.

  • For size exchanges, customers are responsible for return shipping costs.

  • Refunds (if approved) will be processed within 5–7 business days after receiving the returned item.

For all return or refund requests, please contact us first at contact@chaching.co.za before sending any items back.
